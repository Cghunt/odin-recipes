Description

3 Ingredient Peanut Butter Cookies are quick, easy-to-make & tasty.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup - peanut butter
  • 3/4 cup - granulated sugar
  • 1 - large egg

Steps

  1. 1.mix all ingredients in bowl
  2. 2. evenly space spoonfulls on waxed cookie sheet
  3. 3. flatten cookes with bottom of glass
  4. 4. indent top of cookie with fork
  5. 5. Bake 10 minuits in 350 degree oven
  6. 6. let cool
  7. 7. enjoy
